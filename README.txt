Amber Project Summary - Kaitlyn

What: Amber is a health network app with 6 dimentions of health: Spiritual, Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Social, and Financial. Users van view their various health networks in a visualization and receive notifications centered around encouraging human connection.Amber tracks location data, apple health, connects to different social media handels, google apps (gcal/gmail), and integrates AI apps.

Tech stack wise: Amber is built using SwiftUI, Combine, MVVM, iOS 17.0+, Privy.io, Solana, and Togari

Goal: Transition from mock data to real data over the coming weeks
